HISTORY OF JUAN NAVARRO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

In 1954 a class started with 20 pupils taught by Mrs. Purita Abrea under the dilapidated house of Mr. Sergio Cayanan, located along the National Highway Cutcut 1. Due to poor ventilation and sanitary condition of pupils, parent looked for a better place. They secured the permit of the onwer of a rice mill building constructed but not put to completion for some technical and budget problems.

Classes of Grades I and II completed the school year 1955 and 1956 there. Since the building was not adapted for school purpose, civic minded citizens Mr. Sergio Cayanan, PTA President and Mr. Emilio Manio Sr., the captain del Barrio sought a donation of a piece of land where they could put up two-room school building.

The land, MR. JUAN NAVARRO being a civic minded citizen granted a 200 square meters for the request and upon insistence of the late Mrs. Lucia Capitly, the lot was extended 4,600 square meters. The two-room school building was constructed by the parents with the assistance from the municipal funds. Mrs. Domingo and Mrs. A. Manio had to handle multigrade classes I to IV. Pupils had to go to Capas Central School for their Grade V and VI education. Parents realize the safety and time for their children to go to Capas Central School and upon the leadership of Mr. Manio and Mr. Cayanan and the teacher in-charged requested for the intermediate classes. Anouther four rooms were added to the 2-room building and additional teachers were hired for the intermediate. Ms. Remedios Manio, Mr. Abaraham Capity, Mrs. Angelita Mendoza, with the one-one plan.

In 1976, Mrs. Lucia Capitly died, Mrs Remedios Manuo assign as a teacher in-charge, After four years, Mr. Angel Rodriguez was assigned as principal. Then Dr. Angel CJ Rodriguez was followed by several other schools heads: Mr. Vicente Manlapaz, Mr. Eliseo Moyco, Mrs. Adelina Ocampo, Mrs. Norma Macaspac, Mr. Rustico Castro, Mr. Fernando Tabamo, Mrs. Gloria Quebengco, Mrs. Lilian Nicolas, Mrs. Estrella Gomez, Mrs. Caridad Ocampo, Mr. Danilo P. Santos (2013-2018), Mrs. Ethel B. Gonzales (September 2018 - August 2022), and the current school head of Juan Navarro Elementary School is Dr. Mirasol Taruc Valencia, ESGT III.

Throughout the years, Juan Navarro Elementary School has continuously been living to its vision of bringing quality education to all its learners in the service of the humanity, it has evolved that much from a simple school of few learners to a medium school being recognized in the whole province.
