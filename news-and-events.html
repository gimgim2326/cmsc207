Quarterly Science Exhibit

Primary pupils and other stakeholders were in awe as they witnessed the amazing outputs of intermediate learners for the 3rd Quarter in Science. Under Project SAIL (Science Amazing Innovations for Learning), a quarterly science exhibit must be held. This 3rd Quarter a science exhibit was set up to showcase the outputs of the intermediate learners. The activity showed what science skills the learners gained in the said quarter.

The theme for the activity was “Energizing Yourself for an Electrified Result”. The project/activity was headed by Madam Gwyneth A. Payumo, intermediate science teacher and science district area specialist. Through the approval and support of Dr. Mirasol T. Valencia, ESHT III of JNES.

Date: March 22, 2024

JNES Conducts INSET 2024

On January 24 – 26, 2024, Juan Navarro Elementary School (JNES) conducted the School-Based In-Service Training to sustain the capacity of the teachers in providing quality teaching and learning in the classroom.

Various topics were discussed by the Resource Speakers such as: Positive Discipline in Everyday Teaching and Catch-Up Fridays by Mrs. Gwyneth A. Payumo; Strategies to Recover Learning Loses and Ethical Issues Concerning Teaching Profession by Mrs. Rosette C. Aquino; Improving Teachers Competencies in Planning, Assessing and Implementing Through Innovative Programs and Fostering Cultural Awareness and Inclusivity in the Classroom by Mrs. Flora P. Payumo; Upscaling the School Administrators on the Implication of RA 6713 - The Code of Conduct and Ethical Standards for Government Officials and Employees by Dr. Mirasol T. Valencia; Implementing Culturally Responsive Teaching Strategies by Mrs. Arcely B. Miclat; Implementation Guidelines On Strengthening Character Development Through Implementation Of Edukasyon Sa Pagpapakatao (EsP)/Good Manners And Right Conduct (GMRC) and Values Education (VE) by Mrs. Gina R. Estanislao; and School Typhoon

Contingency Plan by Mrs. Erlen C. Reyes. This activity was in pursuant to DepEd Order No. 22, s. 2023 (School Calendar and Activities for School Year 2023-2024) which stated that these days of the mid-year break may be devoted for the conduct of the School-Based In-Service Training (INSET) activities for teachers’ continued professional development and the preparation for their Instructional Materials (IMs).

This INSET is also one of the activities under Project TRAIN (Teachers Respond and Improved Necessary Services) as part of the Enhanced-School Improvement Plan (E-SIP).

Date: January 24, 2024
