Students and Parents

School Clubs and Organizations

The Sports Club is a vibrant community of athletes and fitness enthusiasts.

The Math Club aims to foster a love for mathematics and inspire its members to pursue math-related careers.

The Science Club is a community of students passionate about exploring the wonders of the natural world.

The English Club is a space for literature enthusiasts and aspiring writers.

CLUBS AND ORGANIZATIONS

Diverse range of clubs offers students the opportunity to explore their interests, develop new skills, and build lasting friendships. Each club is a unique and integral part of our school community, fostering an environment of learning, growth, and fun. We encourage all students to get involved, discover new passions, and enhance their elementary school experience.

Parents and Teachers

PTA is a formal organization composed of parents, teachers, and staff that is intended to facilitate parental participation in a school.

The primary role of PTA is to promote communication and collaboration between parents and school staff, and to organize and support activities that enhance the education and well-being of students.

PARENT-TEACHER ASSOCIATION

As dedicated partners in progress, we believe in fostering a nurturing environment that supports our children's growth both academically and personally. Through a variety of programs, events, and initiatives, we aim to build strong relationships between families and the school, ensuring that every child has the opportunity to succeed.

STUDENT HANDBOOK

The student handbook is designed to be a valuable resource, providing you with the essential information you need to navigate your school year successfully. From school policies and procedures to extracurricular activities and academic expectations, this handbook is your go-to guide for understanding the standards and opportunities that make our school unique. We encourage students and parents alike to read through this handbook carefully to become well-acquainted with the life and culture of Juan Navarro Elementary.
